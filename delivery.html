  1. Call Krusty Bucket's hotline (1-800-578-7891) and wait for a staff member to answer the phone.

  2. Kindly provide all necessary contact information asked (i.e., name, address, contact number, supercard details, etc.)

  4. Confirm the details of your order, including the total cost and estimated time for pickup or delivery. Also rovide payment information over the phone or agree upon the payment method upon pickup or delivery.
