About Us

Who are We?

Hello and welcome to the Krusty Bucket! We are a one-of-a-kind culinary combination born by the renowned Krusty Krab and the inventive Chum Bucket. Our objective is to thrill taste senses and satisfy appetites with a menu that combines the finest of both worlds. We create a dining experience that exceeds expectations thanks to SpongeBob's amazing cooking talents, Plankton's innovative flair, and Karen's technology acumen. At the Krusty Bucket, you'll find the ideal blend of tastes, creativity, and a dash of underwater magic.

In the town of Bikini Bottom, the Krusty Krab and the Chum Bucket were bitter rivals until a destructive storm left their establishments in ruins. Seeing an opportunity, Mr. Krabs and Plankton decided to join forces and create a new restaurant called the Krusty Bucket.
They combined their culinary talents and resources to rebuild, merging the Krusty Krab's delicious menu with the Chum Bucket's innovative cooking methods. SpongeBob and Plankton's wife Karen worked together in the kitchen, creating extraordinary dishes that attracted customers from far and wide. The Krusty Bucket became a sensation, offering a fusion of flavors that delighted everyone.
This unexpected collaboration not only transformed the fast food scene in Bikini Bottom but also brought Mr. Krabs and Plankton closer together. The success of the Krusty Bucket taught them the power of unity and showed that even rivals can achieve greatness through collaboration.
